## Overview

The E-Commerce domain owns the checkout. A customer submits an order; the order is
validated, payment is captured, stock is reserved, and a personalized post-purchase
recommendation is generated.

Four services collaborate over three Kafka topics. Each step is traced. Each event
on the wire carries a correlation ID. The catalog you are reading is **the
read-side of the same telemetry** — generated, not maintained.

## Services in this domain

| Service | Role | Emits | Consumes |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[service\|OrdersService]] | Accepts the order, owns the order lifecycle | `OrderPlaced` | `PaymentCaptured`, `InventoryReserved` |
| [[service\|PaymentService]] | Captures payment, retries on failure | `PaymentCaptured`, `PaymentFailed` | `OrderPlaced` |
| [[service\|InventoryService]] | Reserves stock for confirmed orders | `InventoryReserved` | `PaymentCaptured` |
| [[service\|RecommendationsService]] | Generates personalized post-purchase recs via an LLM | `RecommendationGenerated` | `OrderPlaced` |
