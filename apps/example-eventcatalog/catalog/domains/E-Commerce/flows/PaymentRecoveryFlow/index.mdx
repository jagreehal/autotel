## How this flow was generated

This flow was generated from the `payment-failure.integration.test.ts` suite,
which exercises the retry path with a PSP stub that returns `card_declined`
twice then succeeds, and once that returns `card_declined` three times.

<strong>Observed in tests</strong>

<div class="evidence-callout">
<strong>Observed in production</strong>

This flow fired **183 times** in the last 7 days. Of those:

- **2-attempt recoveries**: 142 (78%)
- **3-attempt recoveries**: 31 (17%)
- **Hard failures**: 10 (5%)

The 10 hard failures triggered the recovery email. **6 customers retried
within 24h and succeeded.** Final unrecoverable rate: **2.2%**.

p95 end-to-end (including retries): **2.4s**.
