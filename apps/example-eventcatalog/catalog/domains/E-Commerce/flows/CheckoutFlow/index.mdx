## How this flow was generated

This flow was **generated from the autotel snapshot** produced by the
integration-test suite in `services/test/checkout.integration.test.ts`.

Each `service` step corresponds to an autotel-traced function. Each `message`
step corresponds to a `track()` call or a `traceProducer` publish. The
producer→consumer edges are inferred from autotel's automatic span linking.

### Why this matters

A hand-written sequence diagram of this flow would name the steps. It would
not know:

- That `payment.capture` retries soft declines **2.3% of the time**.
- That the LLM call costs **$0.0005 per recommendation** and uses **412 prompt tokens**.
- That a field called `personalization_seed` started appearing in payloads but is not in the declared schema (see [[event|RecommendationGenerated]]).

That is the gap autotel-eventcatalog closes: the diagram and the runtime are
the same thing.
