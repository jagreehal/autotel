## Overview

`OrderPlaced` is the **fanout event** of the checkout. It is the first thing
written to Kafka once the order is durably persisted, and it triggers the
three parallel reactions: payment capture, recommendation generation, and
(eventually, via payment) inventory reservation.

Produced by [[service|OrdersService]] via `traceProducer` on [[channel|orders.events]].
